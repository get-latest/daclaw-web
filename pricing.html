Pricing FAQ

What is BYO LLM?

Bring Your Own Large Language Model. To give you the best price, we provide the infrastructure and you supply your own API key (like Anthropic or OpenAI) which pays for the actual AI thinking.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Every DaClaw instance runs in its own isolated container. Your API keys and data are encrypted and never shared with other users.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Our plans are month-to-month and you can cancel with one click from your dashboard.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all! We built DaClaw specifically so you can get the power of OpenClaw without touching a terminal, writing code, or managing servers.
