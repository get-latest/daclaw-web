Our Mission

We built DaClaw because we believe AI assistants should genuinely help people — not harvest their data, not lock them into platforms, and not require a PhD to set up.

What is DaClaw?

DaClaw is a managed cloud staffing agency for OpenClaw — the open-source AI assistant that's taking the developer world by storm (80k+ GitHub stars and counting).

Instead of just renting you a server, we provide a fully-trained employee. We handle the infrastructure, the updates, and the maintenance. You get a private, always-on AI assistant that you fully control — without touching a terminal.

Think of us as the easy button for hiring an AI Chief of Staff.

Your Privacy, Non-Negotiable

Every DaClaw instance runs in complete isolation. Your conversations, your files, your data — they never leave your private container.

  • Isolated infrastructure Your instance is physically separated from others.
  • Zero data access We can't read your conversations, even if we wanted to.
  • No AI training Your data is never used to train any model.
  • Encrypted connections End-to-end encryption for all access.

A Note on Independence

DaClaw is an independent service. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the OpenClaw project or its maintainers. We're simply fans of open-source AI who wanted to make it accessible to everyone — without the DevOps headache.
